MovieDict

What do they call it?

Have you ever tried to discuss your favorite films in a hostel abroad? Movie title translations are amazingly creative and often have little to do with the original wording. Who would have guessed that ‘The Big Lebowski’ translates to ‘Murder/Green/Toe’ in Taiwan?

Enter MovieDict, your portable dictionary for ten thousands of popular movie titles.

  • Title translations for over 44,000 movies
  • Shows name of the movie in English, German, French, Russian, Japanese and Chinese
  • Works offline
  • Chinese learners: Look up Chinese characters in the popular Pleco dictionary app
  • Free without ads!
